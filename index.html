About NebulaStudy

NebulaStudy is a free, open-source student productivity tool built to help you focus, stay organised, and get more out of every study session. There's no sign-up, no subscription, and no data collection. Just open it and start working.

Built by a student, for students.

How It Works

NebulaStudy runs entirely in your browser. Your tasks, notes, planner blocks, and timer sessions are saved to your device using localStorage — the same technology browsers use to remember your preferences on any website.

Nothing is sent to a server. Nothing is stored in the cloud. If you clear your browser data, your NebulaStudy data clears with it, so it's worth knowing that going in. There's no account to recover it from.

Privacy

NebulaStudy collects no personal data whatsoever. There are no accounts, no cookies, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services. Everything you enter — tasks, notes, planner schedules — stays on your device.

We have no access to it and never will. The only external request the app makes is loading fonts from Google Fonts when you first open it. If you're offline, your system font is used instead.

Terms of Use

NebulaStudy is provided free of charge, as-is, with no guarantees. You're welcome to use it, share it, and fork it. We're not responsible for any data loss — since everything lives in your browser, clearing your cache or switching browsers will erase your data.

Don't use it as your only record of important information. That's genuinely it — no legal traps, no fine print.
